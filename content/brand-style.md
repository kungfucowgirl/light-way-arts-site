# Brand Style

## Overall feeling
Elegant, warm, artistic, sincere, nonprofit-friendly, culturally rooted but modern.

## Color ideas
- Warm gold
- Deep brown
- Burgundy
- Cream/off-white
- Ink black

## Visual motifs
- Light
- Path
- Lantern
- Shadow
- Paper
- Brushstroke
- Subtle Chinese character inspiration
- Cross-cultural bridge imagery

## Avoid
- Generic tech startup look
- Overly bright red/gold restaurant-style design
- Tourist-style Asian motifs
- Clip-art feeling
- Dense or cluttered layouts

## Logo direction
Possible inspiration:
- Sun and path
- Lantern
- Brushstroke path
- Modern calligraphy
- Chinese characters 光 and/or 伟 as subtle inspiration
