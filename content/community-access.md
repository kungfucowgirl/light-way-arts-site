# Community Access Initiative

## Bringing cross-cultural arts experiences to more families

Light Way Arts Foundation’s Community Access Initiative supports free and low-cost arts, education, and cultural exchange programs for families, students, and community members in Austin and surrounding areas.

Through performances, bilingual storytelling, shadow puppetry, workshops, school and library visits, and community gatherings, the initiative helps make cross-cultural arts experiences more accessible to diverse audiences, including families and young people who may not otherwise have easy access to live performance and arts education.

## What support makes possible

Community support helps us provide:

- Free or reduced-cost access to performances and public programs
- Bilingual storytelling and family arts workshops
- Shadow puppetry, theater, movement, and music activities
- School, library, and community partner visits
- Books, supplies, refreshments, and family-friendly giveaways
- Outreach to families, students, and underserved communities

## Upcoming Initiative: Community Access for Kung Fu Cowgirl

Kung Fu Cowgirl is a cross-cultural musical world blending Texas spirit, Chinese cultural elements, music, movement, humor, courage, and East-meets-West storytelling.

Light Way Arts Foundation is developing community access programming around this work, including family engagement, educational outreach, and opportunities for more Austin-area families and young people to experience cross-cultural live performance.

Support for this initiative can help provide free or reduced-cost access, school and community outreach, bilingual family programming, artist/community engagement, and materials that make the experience more welcoming and accessible.

Learn About Kung Fu Cowgirl: https://www.kungfucowgirl.com/
